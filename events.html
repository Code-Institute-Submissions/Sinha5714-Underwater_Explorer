Join us in our adventure trip

Starting Point

Our journey starts from Berlin Airport

First Destination - Maldives

We are flying to Maldives on 1st April and staying at dive resort in Rasdoo Island for 7 days.
We are diving twice a day for first 5 days and organising beach sports at evenings and on the last day a beach clean-up programme.

Next Destination - Australia

On 9th April, we are flying to Australia and having an dive an world famous- Great Barrier Reef
The stay is 5 days long and again 2 dives a day.

Final Destination- Raja Ampat

Our final destination is every diver's dream. You guessed it right- Raja Ampat, Indonesia.
The stay is 5 days on Liveaboard exploring the marine life and corals. We fly from Australia on 15th April.

Back to home

On 21st April , we will fly back to our home- Berlin
