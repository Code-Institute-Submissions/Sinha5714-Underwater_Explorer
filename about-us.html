About Us


Underwater Explorers is a divers community formed by a group of divers who love to explore various diving spots around the world.
It was formed in 2023 by a group of divers from Berlin.


What do we do?


We conduct trips to various dive destinations around the world. Our fully-qualified instructors also give training to new-divers to who wants to become a certified diver.
We also provide first dive experience to new customers(even to non-swimmers).


Our Team


Our team consists of various certified divers and Instructors who loves to explore different dive spots around the world.
